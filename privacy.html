Privacy Policy for YogBot

At YogBot, your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Public profile information such as name and profile picture.
  • Messages you send to our bot for the purpose of providing responses.

2. How We Use the Information

  • To respond to your questions and provide assistance.
  • To improve the performance and features of our AI assistant.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Data Deletion Process

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. To initiate the deletion process, please send an email to info@kluvor.co with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request and permanently delete all associated data within 24 hours.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at: info@kluvor.co
